About Our Farm

Sustainable Farming Since 2022

Chifwema Farm is a family owned and operated farm dedicated to sustainable agriculture and animal welfare. For over 4 years, we've been cultivating our land with respect for nature and dedication to quality.

Our farm spans 100 acres of lush pastures, crop fields, and natural habitats. We believe in working with nature, not against it, which is why we practice crop rotation, natural pest management, and humane animal care.
